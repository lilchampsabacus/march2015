Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common parent questions

❓

What age can my child start abacus?

+

Children aged 6 years can start abacus training. At this age, they have the fine motor skills and cognitive ability to understand the concept. Early start gives them an advantage across all mathematics.

⏱️

How long is the complete program?

+

The program spans 52 months (approximately 4.4 years) across 10 levels. Each level builds on the previous, creating a comprehensive progression from basic arithmetic to advanced mental mathematics and competition-level problem solving.

📅

What if my child misses a class?

+

We understand life happens! We offer flexible make-up sessions at alternative times. Your child won't fall behind because we ensure they cover all concepts. Just let us know in advance, and we'll schedule a convenient time.

✅

How is progress assessed?

+

We conduct regular assessments at each level to ensure mastery. Progress includes: timed calculation challenges, accuracy tests, formula understanding checks, and competition readiness evaluations.

🏅

Will my child get International certificate?

+

Yes! Every year we have International grading exam at Ambernath conducted by Malaysia Head office. This exam is compulsory for all stduents. SO in 4 years span your child will get minimum 4 International certificates.

🎓

What happens after Level 10?

+

After completing Level 10, students have mastered abacus and mental mathematics to competition standard. Child gets Post Graduation Certificate.

💰

Is there a refund policy?

+

Our fee structure reflects the comprehensive training provided. If you're not satisfied after the free trial class, we can discuss alternatives. However, once enrolled, fees are non-refundable as they cover instructor time, materials, and resources.

🏅

Will my child get competition training?

+

Yes! As part of our regular curriculum, we prepare students for competitions. We have International, National and State level competition once in a year. We have more than 200 winners in competititon.

📱

Is online practice really unlimited?

+

Yes! Absolutely unlimited. With your enrollment, you get access to our entire online practice platform at no additional cost. Practice anytime, anywhere - on laptop, tablet, or phone. No hidden charges ever.
